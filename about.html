A Smarter Way to Plan Your Pilgrimage & Sightseeing in Tirupati

GeoTrip Planner is built by the Geo-Intel Lab at IIT Tirupati under the IITTNIF initiative. It brings together maps, place details, and AI help so you can plan where to go, when to visit, and how to get there — without juggling many apps or websites.

You can get a day-wise plan, see places on a live map, check weather, shortlist temples and attractions, book transport, scan QR at temples, and find nearby hospitals in an emergency — all designed to make your trip safer and smoother.

What We Offer

Sacred Temple Discovery & Darshan Planning

Explore 20+ temples across Tirupati and Tirumala, including the iconic Sri Venkateswara Temple. GeoTrip Planner provides accurate opening hours, visitor ratings, descriptive details, and direct booking links to TTD Devasthanams. Plan your pilgrimage with verified data and never miss a sacred experience.

temple_hindu 20+ Temples schedule Live Timings link TTD Booking

AI-Powered Itinerary & Smart Routing

Let Google Gemini AI craft your perfect day. Based on your selected categories, timings, budget, and group size, the AI generates a fully optimized itinerary. OSRM routing engine calculates the fastest driving routes between your shortlisted places, displayed on an interactive Leaflet.js map with real-time markers and distance estimates.

smart_toy Gemini AI route OSRM Routes map Leaflet.js

Sightseeing & Category-Based Exploration

Browse 116+ curated places across 5 travel categories — Temples, Sightseeing, Food, Adventure, and Wildlife. Each category features time-aware filtering that shows only places open during your selected outing window. The recommendation engine uses TF-IDF cosine similarity to suggest the best matches based on your preferences and proximity.

filter_alt 5 Categories recommend TF-IDF Engine database 116+ Places

Adventure & Outdoor Activities

Tirupati isn't just about temples — discover thrilling trekking trails, fort hikes, and waterfall treks across the Eastern Ghats. GeoTrip Planner maps adventure hotspots with GPS coordinates, visitor ratings, and available timings. Plan your outdoor experience alongside your pilgrimage for a complete travel itinerary.

hiking Trekking terrain Eastern Ghats explore GPS Mapped

Wildlife & Nature Sanctuaries

Explore the rich biodiversity around Tirupati — from Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park to the Deer Park at Tirumala and the Seshachalam Biosphere trails. Our platform includes wildlife destinations with opening hours, visitor ratings, and descriptive details. Experience the natural beauty of the Seshachalam Hills alongside your spiritual journey.

park National Parks pets Wildlife eco Biodiversity

QR Check-in, Budget Splitter & Emergency Support

Every user gets a unique QR code for temple visit tracking. The budget splitter helps groups manage expenses with an intuitive calculator. In emergencies, the hospital locator instantly finds the 3 nearest hospitals with GPS-optimized driving routes. Plus, integrated transport booking for APSRTC buses, local taxis, and IRCTC trains — all from one platform.

Sign Up & OTP Verification

Trip Details & Category Cards

hub Our Lab

Geo-Intel Lab

Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati

The Geo-Intel Lab at IIT Tirupati specializes in geospatial intelligence, location-based services, and AI-driven travel solutions. GeoTrip Planner is a flagship project developed under IITTNIF to enhance the pilgrimage and tourism experience in the Tirupati region through cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission: To leverage geospatial intelligence and artificial intelligence for building smart, accessible, and user-friendly travel planning tools that enhance the pilgrimage and tourism experience in India.
