Technology Innovation Hub (TIH)

IIT Tirupati Navavishkar I-Hub Foundation (IITTNiF)

IITTNiF is IIT Tirupati's dedicated Technology Innovation Hub (TIH) under the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS), funded by the Department of Science and Technology (DST), India. It empowers innovation in Positioning and Precision Technology (PPT) — from foundational research to prototypes and market-ready solutions.

IITTNiF advances translational development across GNSS, GIS, LiDAR, quantum navigation, computer vision, AI/ML, and geospatial systems — while nurturing startups, fellowships, and skill development for sustained technology growth.

Operation Dronagiri

A national initiative highlighted on IITTNiF's platform, driving geospatial innovation across agriculture, infrastructure, and skilling — connecting startups, industry, and academia to real-world challenges in India.

Technology Development

Geo-Intel Lab

Geo-Intel Lab is part of IITTNiF's Technology Development portfolio — alongside translational R&D in GNSS, GIS, LiDAR, quantum navigation, computer vision, and AI/ML. The lab focuses on geospatial tools and intelligent systems that turn location data into decisions people can trust.

GeoTrip Planner is a flagship application from this work: combining accurate coordinates, sensible route ordering, and user-centred design for pilgrims and visitors — aligned with IITTNiF's applied areas such as navigation, smart cities, and disaster-aware planning.
